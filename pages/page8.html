PREFACE

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He couldn’t believe his ears. It was her. With his mixed feelings of comfort and fear, he slowly came out in the hall, thinking whether to respond or play away silently.

‘Please open it Ved. I know you are there.’ as her voice begged in helplessness, she sat down on her knees crying.

He was shocked. Every possible thought crashed in his mind, but his heart still resisted in favor to break the silence, so finally he whispered ‘Disha!’ he paused. ‘How did you get to know that I am here?’

Without waiting for her to reply, he continued in his strained voice. ‘Anyways it doesn’t matter, please go away. And don't tell anyone that I am here, please leave me alone.’

She felt relieved. For her, it was like ages since she had heard his voice.

‘No way, I won’t. It has already been a week now Ved since you went missing. I am so glad I found you.’ She gasped and continued.’ What are you doing here? Please open the door, Let’s go home.’

‘Just go!’ as he talked, Disha noticed his depressed mental state.
