FORTUNATE ENCOUNTER

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Entering the dark, dense forest further, he walked for straight 3 hours in search of any possible archaeological evidence. Following negative output yet again, he finally decided to quit.

Just about he was to retreat when he heard a jingling sound. It came from far somewhere. He followed the sound. Seeing the silhouettes, it was definitely some human walking in the dark. Finding it odd, he chose to follow his shadow.

‘What might he be up to?’ Ved asked himself.

He got a closer look and found that the tall figure to be a male tribal. The man looked stoned as he walked. Stopping near a standing stone, Ved observed him lighting a small lamp near it.

As Ved peeked from the trees, he saw him bowing in moon’s direction and joining his hands to pray. The tribal stood there for a while and then suddenly turned around and gazed at Ved.

His heart skipped a beat, But then Aghori gave a heavenly smile and turned back towards the jungle. For some reason, Ved felt comforting. But he was shocked.

‘Har har Mahadev’ he cheered, praising Neelkanth’s glory, he started walking.
