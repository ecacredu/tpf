FORTUNATE ENCOUNTER

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

With a lot of fear and anxiety, struggling with darkness, adding the uneven rocky and snowy ground, he jumped around and ran as quickly as possible towards the mysterious sculpture and hence the origin of the sound. He felt a sudden rise in temperature as he approached the source.

Finally reached there to find a huge dent in a mountain. The depression was fresh but profound. By applying any human scientific logic, it was not possible. Ved searched for any crashing stones or other elements nearby but couldn’t find any.

After roaming around for a while, he decided to go to the temple above, with the expectation to find something there. He gazed at the marvelous sculpture again. He started trekking upwards, towards the temple. The condition of the monument was devastatingly ruined. It seemed so old that even the mountains had taken the sculpture in its arm.

The reason for the sound was still unknown, but it had made its impact throughout the forest. Ved was not alone to hear it. Tribal apparently had noticed it too. ‘Bluhh bluhh bluhh’ They made recursive howling noise from multiple directions.

Ved was probably the first human to reach there. He suddenly heard a lot of people approaching towards him (cave).
