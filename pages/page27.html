THE FACE OF DEATH

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Disha noticed his cold eyes. Chills went through him even while he was speaking. She couldn’t believe him, but he looked serious.

Not expressing her disbelief but a little disagreement, she said ‘Ved you are alive you see. Please calm down’ She held his hand to comfort him. His hand was still shivering.

‘How did you escape it?’ she asked.

He looked at her with confusion. ‘I don’t know!’ he exclaimed.

He held her hand tightly and suddenly went unstable. ‘I don’t know what happened. I didn’t do anything.'

# # # # #

Snow droplets were hitting him slightly. Closer to the dawn, with his eyes closed, he knew he was going to die. Ved could hear their rush and blood lust. It was loud. And getting louder. He could hear the birds, wolf howls, and other animals. He could hear his heartbeat. He freed his hand from the burden of the torch.

‘This is it’ he thought.
