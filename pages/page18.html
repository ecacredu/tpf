FORTUNATE ENCOUNTER

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He couldn’t believe he saw it. In that massive sculpture, resided a hidden temple. With his eyes, he admired the monument of legends, which showed the mysteries of unknown.

Without wasting any moment, he started walking and eventually running towards it. The fear of it, being a dream and vanishing around, tortured his mind. He couldn’t stop himself.

He was literally flowing towards it. His legs were moving on its own. It drove him so crazy that he forgot about everything and all that he wanted was to go towards it.

This was when he felt something running behind him towards the deeper jungle. His reflexes were sharp. He got alerted. Carefully, he looked behind but found no one.

He sensed an unusual pressure in the atmosphere. It was definitely someone or something. Just when he was trying to analyze the situation when he heard an ear-splitting sound from the other side.

‘Throooooooooooff!!’ The sound was as loud as a meteor crashing from the sky.

As the thing stuck on the ground, the vibrations stumbled him knocking down on the floor.
