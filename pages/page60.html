THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Yug was silent. ‘In my vision, it was not the Granth that killed them.’ Yug continued. ‘I do not know the reason for the same. I saw only darkness and could hear terror. I could not see what murdered them.' Even Yug looked terrified as he remembered the roars. Yug skipped the topic and said. ‘Also, In this universe, there are forces with a lot more strength than you can imagine. If something is sealed and bound, the seal can be broken as well. To express in your language, They have similar mantras to decode it. Don’t underestimate their power.’

‘Oh Brahmin, but you seem very mighty, A simple jump of yours as you said, made a dent on a mountain.’ Ved said. ‘Can someone be stronger than you that you have to run from them?’

‘I was chased by a group, not an individual. Even if I am powerful, I couldn’t have sustained them all. And, it was not about me and my life. God forbid if they had got their hands on the Granth, the world would have turned upside down in even a week till now.’

‘But who are they?’ Ved asked.

‘You will get to know everything. Just wait for the right time.’ Yug said. ‘However, I am glad they did not get their hands on the manuscript.’

Ved was overwhelmed, hearing the power of the book and feared at the same time. ‘But how did I end up getting the Granth? You said the temple was not perceivable by eyes of even those mighty people following you. But I saw it, and that was where I got it. Why me?’
