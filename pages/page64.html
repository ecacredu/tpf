PROFOUND SILENCE

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

‘Because the first human who would get this manuscript was going to be the one.’ Yug said.

‘What does that mean? Even if you had placed it anywhere, any human would have taken it.’ Ved expressed his query.

‘No, it’s not that simple. There are lots of clauses to that as well. Also, you are capable as well. You are intelligent and fearless too.’ Yug replied.

Ved could understand Yug calling him intelligent as a compliment after viewing his research work, but he did not understand the other part. ‘Fearless?’ he asked.

Yug replied. ‘For an ordinary individual, someone mysteriously standing on the ninth floor on his balcony would have been a havoc. But, you reacted quite calmly. You are indeed fearless.’

Yug went speechless, and a pin drop silence spread across the room as he heard what Ved said. ‘Because… I knew you would come.’ Ved continued. ‘I was waiting for you.’
