THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He caressed her hair with his fingers and looked at her eyes. Wiping her tears, he said. ‘I can’t hide it anymore from you. I will tell you everything. Come with me.’

He held her hand caringly. She felt comfortable. They walked towards his research room. Everything seemed messy and disturbed. They walked in front of the cupboard. Signaling her to wait and watch, he opened it's door. Digging inside the stack of his clothes, he took out a big diagonal container. It was packed with a red velvet cloth.

He placed it carefully on his bed beside him. ‘Before I open this, I will tell you everything. But keep it only to yourself?’ He asked reassuringly.

The creepy atmosphere was already very intense. Ignoring the suspense, ‘Yes’ She nodded.

She was curious to know what was buried in his mind for past few days and why he was so over-protective about each and everything that he does and feared each and every person that he met. It was as if, he was afraid of the whole world.

Ved was a well-known Archaeologist. He had won numerous awards, and his contribution towards the society was priceless. For the first time, Disha was seeing him so helpless and disturbed.
