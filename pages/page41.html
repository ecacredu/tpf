THE SECOND MEET

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Keeping all his worries and depression aside, and gathering some courage to speak, joining his hands, asked in a mild tone. ‘Hey Great One, What is it that you seek?’

‘I wish for you to come with me. You have a purpose to serve.’ The sage replied.

With no hesitation, ‘I shall do whatever you say. I will come with you.’ Ved responded in an instant, but he had a lot of unanswered questions which he seeked answers for.

‘You look confused. What is troubling you?’ asked the Brahmin.

‘The situation.’ Ved said.

‘I would be pleased to hear what you have to say.’

Ved then told him the entire story, right from the beginning of his expedition to getting the Scripture. Then seeing his death, the death of his subordinates, and then them getting back to life.

Ved told him every part of what happened. However, the Sage seemed to emphasize on a particular part.

‘You saw the Shiva Temple?’ The sage asked his part of the question and continued. ’That is strange. It can't be seen by everyone. Also, how does your grandfather know about the Verse?’ He asked about the verse, which conveys description, about the Massive Shiva Temple which Ved saw.
