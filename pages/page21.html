FORTUNATE ENCOUNTER

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He was utter less. He had no other thoughts. In that dark stillness, He opened the huge book. Its pages were made up of pure thick tree bark. They seemed to be perfectly engineered so as to survive for ages. The quality of ink and handwritten words itself appeared to be a miracle.

He was glad he wouldn't be going home empty handed. He was happy. ‘Sometimes all it requires is dedicated and honest effort’ he thought. There are a lot of times when we strive for something and quit. But we try harder further and get extraordinary results.

‘Mahabharata!’ He cheered loudly. ‘I have found The Mahabharata.
