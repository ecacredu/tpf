EXTENDED MORTALITY

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Ved knew it would be offensive, but if it were a situation of life or death, it didn’t matter. ‘Oh brahmin, Why are we not sure of me getting the answers?’

‘Because some things are beyond our knowledge as well.’ Yug said.

‘But at least the one who predicted that the first human who gets his hands on the manuscript would be the one, should know the purpose of the one right?’ Ved asked confusingly.

‘Yes. He should. But it is not the head of my clan who predicted that.’ Yug clarified.

‘Then?’

‘It is Maharishi Ved Vyas’ Yug replied.
