THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He was shocked. All the forgotten memories came back to life, and his facial excitement turned to grief and fear. ‘I didn’t do anything Disha. I don’t know anything how it all happened.’ He mumbled.

She was still confused. She saw sudden stillness. Ved had no practical reason to be so depressed. ‘What happened? You tell me, why are you hiding here in this old apartment?’

He looked at her. He had turned sweaty. But he continued.

He couldn’t believe it. In that dense dark cave, his cheer echoed everywhere. He was suddenly energized. With a lot of excitement, he started turning those grubby pages. He was amazed and excited. The content inside was not as understandable as the title, though. The language used was ancient and would need additional know-how. He tried to push it into his traveler backpack. The book was too big to fit in with current items.

He tried harder to force it in but was unsuccessful.
