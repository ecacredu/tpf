CHAPTER

FIVE

THE PERFECT DECISION

Ved was disappointed. All his excitement and enthusiasm was drained all of a sudden. The Brahmin standing in front of him was not whom he expected him to be.

‘Then this manuscript…’ Ved continued pleading. ‘Is it not Mahabharata?’

‘No.’ ‘It is my clan’s treasure. We can summon Maharishi Vyas if we have this manuscript.’ Said the sage.

‘But the title, Granth Vyas?’

‘That name has a deep history. I will tell you in depth some day. It is not Mahabharata itself, but you can consider it a derivative.’ Yug said looking at Ved’s disappointed face.

He further continued. ‘I apologize for your disappointment. But The Mahabharata is too vast to be compiled in a single book and meeting Maharishi is not that easy. It has been more than five centuries since even I am myself searching for him.'
