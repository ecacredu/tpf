THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Ved was alarmed. ‘You mean I saw, what I was going to see on the next day?’ He said.

‘As I said, it was neither reality nor an illusion. It was a glimpse of a possible future.’ Yug said smilingly. Looking at terrified Ved, he continued ‘Which you avoided by your decision to believe that the power of the manuscript is above human intervention.’
