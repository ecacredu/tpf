After taking some rest, he got fresh and said to Steve. ‘I have something to show to you guys. Call everyone Steve’

Steve followed his team leader's orders and did the same. The most he thought about how he can help in the situation was to listen to what Ved was saying.

Meanwhile, Ved called his lawyer and told him that he was playing a prank on him and that he did not need to worry. He even made Steve talk to him. He was convinced that it was a joke, and they had their part of laughs. Things seemed to line up pretty well as time was passing.

As everyone came to the room, they looked at Ved as he opened his handbag and took the manuscript out of it. ‘This is what I found yesterday.’ He said.

He told them the entire story of how he started his expedition from old ruins, then just for the sake of it went a bit ahead to find the old Aghori who mysteriously smiled at him. Then, the majestic view Shiva sculpture and hence the temple. The ear-splitting sound, him finding the book and hence the wild tribal running, towards him to kill him.

‘But how did you escape it?’ Steve asked. ‘How did you reach here? It’s practically impossible bro.’

‘I don’t know.’ Ved said. ‘Also, doesn’t matter now. I am here’ he said asking him to ignore it.
