THE PERFECT AUTHOR

Maybe I should stop my work, he thought as Disha was waiting for him. He decided to reserve the research work for later and commence for home.

Just when he was about to get up. He felt someone standing behind him in the balcony. This feeling was similar to one he felt in his dream cum reality and when he had heard the jolting sound near the standing stone lit with lamp. He instantly turned back with expectations to find no one again, but this time he was really there.

The dominant dark figure in the moonlight was looking dreadful but comforting. The door was closed, and there was no other way for a person to come other than windows or balcony.

Standing in his balcony on 9th floor. Dressed as an ancient Hindu sage, the mysterious dark figure in silhouettes said in a calm, comforting voice

‘This book belongs to me.’
