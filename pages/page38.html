UNEXPECTED REVEAL

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He looked at his current research progress. He then immediately called several people from the University and sent them samples for decoding. There were strange things about the book. The writing seemed to be written in multiple slots of time. One slot he even estimated to be more than 3 centuries old and others were relatively as fresh as hundred years. It looked as if someone was still writing the book.

This acknowledged the myth that the author of Mahabharata, ‘Maharishi Ved Vyas’ is assumed to be immortal. Was it really Vyas? Or maybe a hereditary generation to generation passing of the book, who were continuing to write the literature. But why would anyone continue to write Mahabharata? Is it really Mahabharata?

But, to contradict this point, as per his handwriting analysis, he was stunned. It seemed to be entirely written by the same person. The report also displayed personal traits like fearlessness and freedom. It was really strange.

If his research went successful and if he could prove that the book is actually Mahabharata, then it could put light on a lot of facts about Hindu mythology.

These things were too messy to be thought of together. He looked at his wrist watch. It was already night. He wanted to research more, but in the cloudy weather, the rainy thunders were distracting him.
