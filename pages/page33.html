THE PERFECT AUTHOR

His grandfather’s place was safe. It was locked for several years, and only he had the keys for the same. He locked himself there as nobody will notice him. Continuing to live in fear, he started research work on the book.

Nobody knew about that flat as he was an adopted son and had no documentation for the same. His parents left him alone very soon, and he was raised by his grandfather who was also an archaeologist. Nobody except for his close friend Disha knew about this place.

‘I know Disha, it’s hard to believe, but I seriously didn’t do anything.’ Ved struggled. ‘Please trust me.'

Disha was startled. She got hold of matter. She actually got answers to all her questions together on why he was behaving strangely.

‘Just a moment Ved’ She took out her phone. She started dialing some number.

‘Please don't call the cops. Nobody would believe me. I am sure of it.’ Ved pled, but couldn’t stop her.
