THE FACE OF DEATH

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

He told her that according to his knowledge Mahabharata was narrated by Maharishi Vyas in 3rd to 4th BCE.

‘Are we serious?’ asked Disha. ‘What does the book say? Is it really it?’

‘I don’t know. The script is too old to understand or interpret that easily.’ he said.

‘Also just look here.’ He slightly rubbed over the cream colored grungy page. ‘This exceptional quality; As per my knowledge, these are handmade. Also just see the quality of ink. The chemical formulation is so right that it will easily sustain another century.’

She was just looking at him. Disha believed in such things and already was convinced for it to be Mahabharata. She asked ‘When will you be able to translate it?’

She thought for a while. ‘This will be one of the world’s most treasured scripts ever!’ she exclaimed.

She gazed at him as he looked at her with his cold eyes. ‘Why are you so stressed. It should be a happy moment, shouldn’t it?’ She came back to her original question.
