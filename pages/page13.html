CHAPTER

ONE

FORTUNATE ENCOUNTER

‘Come fast, Ved.’ called his colleague.

Their clothes were all dusty. Everyone was tired of seeing those thick rigid rocks and hence the expedition. Ved being the team leader, felt nervous as he felt the responsibility for the whole trip and hence its results, which were not up to the mark. They were all here for the sake of curiosity, expecting something from the old ruins of ancient Indian culture. But after 15 days of collective work, they were empty handed.

Ved and his reforms were well known, and he was a renowned archaeologist of all times. He had won countless awards. This expedition was supposed to be best of his expectations.

They had already called the day off. But considering his persistent nature, he was still inside giving a last glance at everything to see if they missed anything.

‘Yes Steve. Coming...’ He said sadly while coming out of the cave. ‘I guess we have to go back empty-handed this time.’ He said to his trio subordinates.
