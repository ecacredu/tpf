CHAPTER

THREE

UNEXPECTED REVEAL

‘What have you done?’ looking at the cursed book, he yelled it.

There was no scope of him not being blamed for what had happened. He had no shred of doubt that he will either go behind bars or will be sentenced to death. All his reputation, his awards now seemed powerless. He knew he would lose it all.

His common sense told him to leave. He looked at the book and decided to leave it aside. He got hold of his bag and found it to be very light.

‘It’s empty’ he thought.

He remembered that he had put out all the stuff along with his passport in that cave. ‘I was there. It was real.’ He thought as all those horrifying memories came back together. He also got sight of those wildings approaching him.

‘Should I take it with me or not?’ He thought while looking at the book.
