‘Did someone just clean the suite?’ Ved said as he saw a blurry vision in front of him. ‘I didn’t do anything’ he mumbled and felt unconscious.

……… … ...

‘Ved! Ved get up bro!’ said a familiar voice. ‘What have you done to yourself?’ Ved heard as he came back to consciousness.

He was on his bed, surrounded by the manager, his subordinates and a person with a stethoscope hanging around his neck.

‘He hasn’t eaten since yesterday. Also, dehydrated. It is certain; he will be weak.’ Said the doctor.

‘We will take care of him, Doctor.’ Steve said. ‘We had our flight back today to the US, but guess we’ve missed it.’

‘Well, he will be okay now.’ Said the doctor. He then talked with others a bit, gave necessary instructions and got out of the room.

After some time, Ved got up and got hold of the situation. ‘It was just an illusion’ he declared to himself. They are live and alive.

‘Hey… You should rest! And next time you are not going anywhere alone.’ Said his roommate, rushing back to him quickly.

‘Yes, I won't.’ He said remembering his death scene.
