THE PERFECT AUTHOR

It was a hard time walking. Ved was continuously haunted by the vision of the subordinates. He reached the reception to find the manager sitting there.

‘Hello,’ he shivered as he spoke. ‘Could you please come with me to my room. I have something show to you.’

Manager busy with his call earlier looked at him, seemed shocked but relieved. ‘Sir you are here? Where did you go by yourself yesterday night?’

Ved was shocked. He was speechless. He preferred to say nothing.

The manager addressed his discomfort. He chose to divert the topic. ‘Your room sir? Also, others are searching for you since yesterday? Have you talked to them?’

He again chose to remain silent as he knew no longer can they search for him. ‘Please come with me.’

Ved and the manager started walking back to the room. Ved also insisted two other staff members, to come with them.

As they reached the room, Ved was traumatized. ‘Am I possessed?’ Ved thought. There was nothing. Everything was clean and neat. No bloodshed. No dead bodies.
