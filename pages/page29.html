THE FACE OF DEATH

THE PERFECT AUTHOR

‘AAAAAhhh’ He screamed. Every cell in his body was horrified.

Chills went through him as he saw Steve, Hussain, and Peter lying down beside his bed. They had ruthlessly been murdered. They were headless, lying in blood. Blood was spilled everywhere on walls and throughout the room. Their heads were mercilessly thrown beside them.

They were not only beheaded, but it seemed like someone pulled their heads of their spine. Ved went thoughtless for some time.

It was shocking. Ved didn’t know what to do. Just when he was feeling relieved from the fearful dream, he was unknowingly facing another terror. He screamed again.

With fear, he slid back towards the wall on the bed, without landing down. The room itself smelled dead. Looking at the speechless room, He questioned himself. ‘What the hell just happened?’

He turned to his left and was shocked.

His senses went dumb as he saw an old gold titled book with a warm shine. Besides the bed, it was placed carefully on the table. It seemed to have no problem with things going on as it is. With words carved on it in ancient Sanskrit.

‘Granth Vyas’ it read.
